Privacy Policy

Stage Mate, Session Remote & Stage Lyrics
Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications developed by Andrzej Bandosz:

  • Stage Mate — Multitrack live performance player for iPad
  • Session Remote — Wireless remote control for Stage Mate (iPhone & Mac)
  • Stage Lyrics — Wireless lyrics display for iPad

None of these apps collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All data (playlists, songs, settings, lyrics) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and local files). No data is sent to any external server or third party.

Network Usage

Session Remote uses Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework to communicate with Stage Mate over a local Bluetooth or Wi-Fi connection. No data leaves your local network.

Stage Lyrics uses Apple's Bonjour protocol for automatic discovery of Stage Mate on the local Wi-Fi network. Lyrics are transmitted only within your local network. No data is sent to the internet.

None of the apps require an internet connection to function.

Files & Folders

Stage Mate and Stage Lyrics access audio files and lyrics folders only when explicitly selected by the user via the iOS file picker. No files are uploaded, shared, or transmitted outside the local network.

Analytics & Tracking

No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used in any of these applications. Your usage data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

These apps do not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
